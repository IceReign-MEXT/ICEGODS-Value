ICEGODS WHITEPAPER
==================

1. Introduction
---------------
ICEGODS is more than a bot; it is a movement for transparency, empowerment, 
and protection in the crypto space. Built from personal struggle and vision, 
this project aims to expose hidden practices, provide tools for ordinary users, 
and safeguard digital wealth for the community.

2. The Story
------------
Mex Robert’s journey began with challenges in the crypto space, where many 
developers exploited the poor. Over 13 months, he independently built a suite 
of tools, including bots, dashboards, and AI assistants, to empower users and 
track blockchain activity securely. This project represents the culmination of 
those efforts, a living legacy to protect future generations.

3. Mission & Vision
------------------
- Expose unfair practices in crypto trading and development.
- Provide accessible tools to protect wealth and track assets.
- Empower the community through transparency and AI-assisted monitoring.
- Create a lasting legacy that can manage and safeguard resources 
  even for unborn generations.

4. ICEGODS EchoEyes Bot
-----------------------
- Real-time monitoring of wallet activity.
- Commands for stats, subscriptions, deposit wallets, and confirmations:
    /start - Show menu
    /stats - Check system status and uptime
    /whitepaper - Access this document
    /subscribe <plan> - Subscribe to a plan
    /confirm <tx> - Confirm payments
    /wallets - List deposit wallets
    /status - Check subscription status
